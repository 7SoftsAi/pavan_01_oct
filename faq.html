Why Choose Us?
What are the admission requirements for the school?

The admission requirements vary by grade level. Generally, students must provide proof of age, previous academic records, and a completed application form. For specific details on each grade, please visit our admissions page or contact the admissions office.

What extracurricular activities are offered at the school?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports teams (soccer, basketball, volleyball), arts programs (painting, music, dance), academic clubs (math club, science club), and more. These activities are designed to complement academic learning and encourage personal growth.

What is the school's homework policy?

Our homework policy is designed to reinforce classroom learning. Homework is assigned regularly but is kept at an appropriate level for each grade. We encourage students to balance homework with other activities, and our teachers are always available to provide extra help if needed.

How can parents stay informed about their child’s progress?

Parents can stay informed through regular parent-teacher meetings, access to the school’s online portal, and report cards issued each term. Teachers are also available for consultations via email or phone to discuss any concerns or updates about a student's progress.
